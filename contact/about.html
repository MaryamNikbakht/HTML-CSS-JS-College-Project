I love science and Mathematics,I love painting and photography. I love everything about life and enjoying it. Using photo imagery, drawing and collage as starting points, my stitched textile and mixed media work has evolved in many different directions over the years.

I welcome commissions from people looking to transform a favourite photograph into a painting, and will endeavour to capture the essence of each image with my own style. My main focus is portraiture, however I enjoys approaching a range of subjects.
